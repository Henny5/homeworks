Menu

At Fireside Restaurant you will find a kosher, seasonal, creative and varied glat menu, using fresh, high-quality ingredients. All the dishes, from the breads and dips to the desserts are homemade - the freshest and finest food imaginable.

About Us

Fireside is one of the unique restaurants in all of Jerusalem. Diners will enjoy the spectacular city views from the roof of the hotel, which provide an unforgettable experience. The restaurant is a pleasant and intimate meeting place with the option to sit inside or outside. The dishes are seasonal, with an emphasis on the daily special. The Fireside restaurant is under the strict supervision of the OU Glatt Mehadrin and the Mehadrin Rabbinate, and all the meats are exclusively in the elegant kosher of the Haredi conference.
